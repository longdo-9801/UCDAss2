Description

The standard issue riffle of the Vietnam People Army. The gun was design by Soviet comrade Kalasnikov for the Red Army and through Russia was given to Vietnam. By the time of the Ho Chi Minh Campaign, the VPA have fully mastered the ability to manufacture this weapon on our own and fully issueing it to every soldier, phasing out older weapons such as SKS rifles and PPSh sub-machinegun.
